Why studying psychology???

Studying psychology initially felt that this major was very meaningful and interesting, which could make me become a person who understands others, can help others solve problems, and even make money. When I really go to learn, I found that, in fact, no matter whether I have the ability to help others, psychology is a huge harvest for myself. By studying psychology, I will learn to pay more attention to my own feelings and knowledge, and think more about what is related to my real thoughts, rather than living for others. By studying psychology, I have become more tolerant and have a better mentality. Can accept the bad side of the world, can accept good, can accept evil, can be good to others, can also accept others bad. It made me stronger. I became stronger inside because I could face setbacks head-on. Even in my heart, there is no "frustration" the concept, all the things that look not so smooth, are to test me, exercise me, let me enrich, let me grow. Therefore, I am not afraid of anything and will not escape

Choosing psychology is a thing that will benefit you for life, you can learn to adjust your ideas, while not going against your heart, so that people see some things more simple, see more transparent, life becomes heavy and simple. Psychoanalysis from psychology allowed me to explore my own childhood trauma and my unconscious, cognitive psychology allowed me to discover my bad automatic thinking, and learning metacognition allowed me to focus more. Through the study of these knowledge, I understand myself better than before and become more transparent. 2.
